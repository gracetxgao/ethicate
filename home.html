what is ethicate?

Ethicate is a web app built to help young children track their deeds and grow into morally and ethically sound people.

who is this for?

This tool was built to assist elementary school teachers in promoting good habits among students.

who are we?

We are a group of high school students passionate about creating change in the world through our passions for computer science
